Recent News

We recently discussed some recent developments in our company with the founder and CEO of DMS, Frank Peña, with the focus of our discussion primarily exploring his work alongside co-creator and teaching partner Ed Bell in putting together a Gas Industry Class at the University of Houston. Their work was recently highlighted in an article for the Energy Metro Desk. The class—which has seen an enormous amount of success since its inception in the spring of 2010, with enrollment topping well over a hundred students in the most recent semester—primarily focuses on giving the students hands-on information and the opportunity to work with the actual programs used within the gas industry.


It is the only class of its kind, and down the road Frank hopes to add supplementary classes to the current one. Frank explained that it was his vision early on in his career to create a class applicable to the various fields in the gas industry by “focusing on what the industry truly needed—new recruits who could be an asset from day one.” As evidenced by the success of the class, that vision is quickly becoming a reality.
