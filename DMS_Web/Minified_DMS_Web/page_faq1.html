GasPro's Features

> Real-Time Interfaces

We offer connectivity to ICE, Clearport, Inter- & Intra-State EDI, Platts, GL Uploads and SCADA.

> Physical / Financial Trading

GasPro provides several easy-to-use physical and financial trading tools, from screens that track daily deals, allow multiple simultaneous updates, and help manage your available swing volumes, to an easy to use hedge function. The Trading section has all the tools your traders will need including:

Confirmations, Position Management, Futures, Swap, Options, Mark-to-Market and Settlement.

- Scheduling

GasPro provides an array of scheduling tools, including screens that track transactions from pipeline and/or point level views. Schedulers have all the information they need to make quick and timely scheduling decisions, including real-time volumetric MDQ, imbalance, and storage positions. With our EDI nominations process, transmitting the scheduling information is just a click away.

- Actuals

GasPro provides powerful, yet easy to use tools for actualizing and re-allocating your volumes. From screens that allow you to setup pre-determined allocations, to daily and/or monthly allocations, GasPro has all you need to make the actualization process simple.

- Storage and Imbalances

We provide an array of tools that assist with storages and imbalances, including Real-Time Position, Rachets and Cash-Outs.

> Controls

We offer a comprehensive set of controls including the following:

Sarbane-Oxley Act: Preventive / Detective / Corrective Measures
Security: Screen Level / Simple / Effective Management
Alerts & Advisories: Event Driven / User or Group Specific

> Compliance

The GasPro system is designed to accommodate controls and reporting changes brought about by new clients, industry changes, and regulatory requirements. With over 20 years of providing its clients with the audit information required to comply with their individual, and at times unique audit requirements, DMS has proven its ability to meet the FERC, FAS, and SOX controls and reporting mandates.

- Limits

Enhanced Credit Limit tracking/reporting with the ability to export information to an external Credit Scoring system. Ability to manually set Credit scores and limits. Ability to define Trader Limits by dollars, transactions, net position, etc., and the ability to establish Management Overrides

- Tolerances

Define operating tolerances with the ability to create email notifications/alerts to multiple parties whenever a tolerance is exceeded.

- Auditing

Our super Auditors (by section) are capable of tracking any change made to any field for any reason. Compares what was actually modified to the change reason provided. Created based on our Ad Hoc functionality to allow for a multitude of search criteria.

- Policies

Ability to define and store various policies in GasPro allowing for easy verification during trading periods.

- Security

Enhanced User Security taking control beyond the screen level all the way down to functionality within a given screen. Also the ability to create User Groups for easy maintenance and configuration of multiple users.

- Alerts

Notification system using either internal GasPro emails, external emails, or both to provide information on situations that need action. This will include such issues as exceeding tolerances, exceeding limits, exceeding MDQ, out-of-balance situations, new transactions available to link, and notifications based on timeliness of information input..

- Training

DMS is implementing a video training system where users will be able to access a website and navigate to a short (5-10 min) video containing detailed information about various aspects of the GasPro system. A Knowledge Base is also being implemented to aid in researching methods other clients have used to perform certain business tasks.

> Gas Accounting

The GasPro accounting process is fully automatic. Every transaction entered by the Front Office is captured and seamlessly recorded in the gas accounting system within GasPro. The process can be extended to flow the information to a corporate financial system. GasPro provides advanced functionality to record, control, and analyze every accounting transaction.

- Rates / Indexes

GasPro provides a very powerful Rate Calculator which maintains all pricing information required by any transaction. This calculator is capable of handling complex pricing including collars, tierings, multi-level component rates, flats, spots, higher/lower, averages, index plus, etc. GasPro also tracks both monthly and daily indexes for use in rate calculations.

- Accounts Payable

GasPro tracks and maintains your current accounts payable position. Based on Payments scheduled and posted, it will determine your current 30/60/90 day agings and has the ability to upload this information to corporate level accounting systems.

- Vouchers

GasPro calculates all vouchers in real-time allowing you to easily track and verify any invoices you receive from 3rd parties which will considerably reduce the time required to reconcile.

- Payments

GasPro allows you to schedule, authorize and process payments directly from the system with the ability to upload this information to a corporate level accounting system.

- Accounts Receivable

GasPro tracks and maintains your current accounts receivable position. Based on Cash received and posted, it will determine your current 30/60/90 day agings and has the ability to upload this information to corporate level accounting systems.

- Invoices

GasPro calculates and creates all invoices in real-time allowing you to bill all your customers on a timely basis.

- Cash

GasPro allows you to receive and post cash directly from the system with the ability to upload this information to a corporate level accounting system.

- GL Entries

GasPro allows for manual Gl adjustments to be made which provides a more robust accounting system capable of tracking your transactions at any level your corporation requires.

> Reporting

GasPro provides the most powerful user defined ad hoc query and reporting tools in the industry, as well as over 150 industry standard reports.

- Ad Hoc Reports

In each major section of GasPro, an Ad Hoc style report has been created to allow the user to create very complex and customized queries that they can both run and save (to be run again at a later time). These reports all have multi-select capabilities on numerous filters, plus user-defined sorts and subtotals. The combination of all these filters and sorts provides the user with the capability to create over 5,000 different report configurations (per GasPro section). All of the ad hoc reports also have the capability to extract their results via a standard CSV file format (which can be easily read by Excel, Lotus, Access, etc.)

- MDQ Available

GasPro tracks your current MDQ position in real-time and provides a graphical display showing the Total MDQ, the volumes that have been released, the volumes scheduled and the remaining MDQ. Scheduled volumes are also broken down by transaction type (firm, interruptible, pooling, etc.) A simple double-click on any part of the graph will display the detailed report behind the numbers.

- Balanced Position

GasPro tracks your current balance position (both Storage and Imbalance) on a pipeline, contract and point basis. A graphical display is created to show total balances, change in balance by day and percentage of total balance by type (standard, OBA, 3rd party, etc).

- Rate View

GasPro provides a graphical chart of two user-selected rates allowing for a quick visual determination of which pricing would be the better choice at any given time.

- Variances

GasPro maintains and tracks point level variances for both scheduling and actuals. Detailed reports with simple double-click drill down capability, make it easy to determine exactly where and why any variances occur. Views are provided at the pipeline and at the point level and both views provide both a daily and a monthly selection.

- Credit Limits

GasPro maintains and tracks the current Credit Limit exposure by 3rd party in real-time. Each time a transaction is entered the appropriate credit limit is updated and tracked. Detailed reports with double-click drill down allow you to easily track and determine the credit limit of any entity at any time.

- Predefined Reports

In addition to the ad hoc reports, GasPro also has over 150 pre-defined reports that cover all the standard reporting functions. Many of these pre-defined reports also have multi-select filtering and user-defined sorts and subtotals.
