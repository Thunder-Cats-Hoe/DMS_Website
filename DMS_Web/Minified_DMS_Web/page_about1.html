About DMS

The founders of Data Management Solutions (DMS) are professionals within the gas industry. Their experience developing several major pipeline systems provided the sound foundation on which to create a company to address the functional requirements not being fulfilled by available ETRM Gas Systems on the market.

Since 1993, DMS has been successfully providing commercially supported ETRM software solutions to a broad spectrum of industry participants in the North American Natural Gas markets.

The DMS team has experience in every facet of the gas industry including trading, scheduling, EDI nominations, imbalances, storage, volume allocations, gas processing, settlement, and internal, financial, and regulatory reporting.

“We can tell you about the software and the team support all day long, but when our clients do the talking for us, that’s when you know you’ve become a valued member of their team. A life-long partner, not just another vendor. A problem solver, not just an order taker.”

CEO, J. Frank Pena

.....Over 20 years ago, a small group of pipeline, accounting and mathematic experts gathered together to take on an impending natural gas industry challenge… find a better way, a seamless way, to manage the business functions of companies operating in this emerging sector. The company they formed was Data Management Solutions (DMS).

.....The solution they created was GasPro, an integrated gas management and trading software system providing transparency and efficiencies across the front, middle, and back offices. Today, DMS’ GasPro solution maintains a 100% Implementation Success Rate and is hard at work for prominent Regulatory Agencies, Marketing, Producer Services and Local Distribution Companies across the country.

About The Founder

Frank Peña, C.P.A. and CEO of DMS, has been in the gas industry for 25 years.
In 1993, he founded DMS to meet the needs within the industry to address the requirements that were not being fulfilled by the available gas management systems on the market.

Experience

Frank joined Northern Natural Gas Company (NNG) in 1990 as Manager of Customer Service. In this position he created and structured a control group that was non-existent at NNG previously. Together they designed, developed, and implemented controls in several critical areas including financial, transportation imbalances, gas storage underground, fuel consumption, regulatory and internal/external reporting requirements.

Knowledge

Mr. Peña has testified before the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC) proceedings as an expert witness on gas accounting and Purchase Gas Adjustment (PGA) issues. He has also co-chaired the Southern Gas Association on two separate occasions, once representing United Gas Pipeline (1989) formerly, now Koch Industries and the second time Northern Natural Gas Pipeline (1991).

Committment

Currently he is furthering his passion for the industry by donating his time and resources to the industry's future leaders. He has trained hundreds of college graduate and undergraduate students at University of Houston's C.T. BAUER COLLEGE of BUSINESS.
